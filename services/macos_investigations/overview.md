# Service Overview
macOS Investigations provide rapid triage and deep-dive analysis for Apple endpoints to understand suspected malicious activity, data exposure, or policy violations so customers can quickly contain risk on macOS fleets.

## Typical Customers
- Mid-market organizations expanding Apple deployments
- SaaS companies with mixed device fleets
- Security teams needing macOS-specific expertise

## Scope
- Host-based forensic collection and artifact review on macOS systems
- Timeline reconstruction of user, process, and network activity
- Identification of persistence, credential access, and lateral movement attempts
- Coordination with IT to isolate affected devices

## Out of Scope
- Windows or Linux endpoint analysis beyond basic pivoting
- Mobile (iOS/iPadOS) device forensics
- Full eDiscovery or legal hold management
- Enterprise-wide incident command for multi-platform intrusions

## Inputs
- List of impacted hosts and relevant timestamps
- Access to endpoint management tools (e.g., MDM) for collection
- Any known indicators, user reports, or suspicious artifacts
- Point of contact for coordination and approvals

## Outputs
- Investigation summary and detailed findings per host
- Evidence package of collected artifacts and parsed timelines
- Recommended containment and remediation actions for macOS
- Readout session to review conclusions and next steps
