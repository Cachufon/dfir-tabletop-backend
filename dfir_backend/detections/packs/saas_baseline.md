# SaaS Baseline Pack

Use this pack for SaaS-focused detections across collaboration and productivity platforms.

- Reference `dfir_backend/rules/sigma/` for SaaS-related rules.
- Typical categories: inbox forwarding, mass downloads, external sharing, OAuth anomalies, and detections for platforms like Slack or GitHub.
- Augment with tenant-specific rules under `dfir_backend/custom/<case_id>/` as required.
