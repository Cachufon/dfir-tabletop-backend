# Executive Summary Template

## Purpose
- Engagement objectives and decision context
- Stakeholder priorities and intended audience

## Key Questions Answered
- List of questions the investigation addressed
- Confirmed and out-of-scope items

## High-Level Findings
- Brief descriptions of key findings
- Supporting metrics or evidence at a glance

## Risk Summary
- Business impact and exposure
- Likelihood and urgency
- Notable dependencies or constraints

## Top Recommendations
- Highest-value actions prioritized
- Ownership and due dates

## Next 7 / 30 / 90 Day Actions
- Immediate (7 days): rapid containment and monitoring steps
- Near term (30 days): remediation and validation activities
- Strategic (90 days): hardening, process, and program improvements
