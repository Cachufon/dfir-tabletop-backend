# Finding Template

## Finding ID
- Unique identifier for tracking and cross-referencing

## Title
- Short, descriptive name of the finding

## Category (Identity / SaaS / Endpoint / Executive)
- Classification to group related findings

## Description
- Concise narrative of what was observed
- Timeline highlights and affected assets

## Evidence Summary
- Key artifacts, logs, and screenshots
- How the evidence supports the finding

## Impact
- Potential or realized business effect
- Data exposure, operational disruption, or compliance risk

## Likelihood / Confidence
- Assessment of likelihood and confidence level
- Rationale and assumptions

## Severity
- Severity rating with criteria

## Recommended Actions
- Containment, remediation, and validation steps
- Ownership and expected completion date

## Related Data Sources
- Logs, datasets, or tools used to validate the finding

## ATT&CK Mapping
- Relevant MITRE ATT&CK techniques and sub-techniques
- Detection and mitigation references
