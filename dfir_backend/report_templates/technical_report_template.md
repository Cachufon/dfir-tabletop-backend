# Technical Report Template

## Title Page
- Engagement title and client
- Date range
- Authors and reviewers
- Version history

## Executive Summary
- Plain-language overview of what happened and current risk
- Key quantified outcomes and business impact
- One-slide-ready summary bullets

## Scope
- Systems, accounts, and business units included
- Timeframe covered
- Out-of-scope items

## Methodology
- Investigation phases and approach
- Tools, queries, and automation leveraged
- Key assumptions

## Data Sources
- Logs, telemetry, and repositories consulted
- Collection methods and retention windows
- Data quality considerations

## Detailed Findings
- Individual findings with evidence and timelines
- Detection and containment status
- Cross-references to appendices and artifacts

## Blast Radius
- Assets, identities, and data affected
- Lateral movement and pivot opportunities
- Estimated exposure and business functions impacted

## Remediation Recommendations
- Immediate containment actions
- Short- and medium-term fixes
- Long-term hardening steps and ownership

## Limitations
- Gaps in visibility or access
- Constraints that may affect confidence levels
- Open questions pending additional data

## Appendices
- Timeline of events
- Query snippets, screenshots, and artifacts
- Glossary and references
