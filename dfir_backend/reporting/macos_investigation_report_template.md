# INTERNAL - macOS Investigation Report Template (Defensible Skeleton)

## Investigation Overview
Brief summary of the request, objectives, and investigation type.

## Scope & Legal Context
State custodians, devices, approvals, legal hold status, and any HR/Legal constraints that shaped the approach.

## Methodology
Describe acquisition and analysis steps performed, tools or data classes involved (placeholder only), and how chain-of-custody was maintained.

## Device & User Context
Outline device identifiers, user accounts, roles, and any relevant environmental details.

## Timeline of Activity
Chronological narrative of notable events with timestamps and sources; distinguish observations from interpretations.

## Findings
Neutral, audience-appropriate statements mapped to categories; include supporting evidence references without embedding raw artifacts.

## Evidence Summary (Appendix)
Indexed list of collected artifacts, hashes where applicable, storage locations, and chain-of-custody notes.

## Conclusions
Synthesize findings, call out residual risk or unanswered questions, and note alignment to objectives.

## Limitations
Document scoping limits, data gaps, environmental constraints, and assumptions to preserve defensibility.
