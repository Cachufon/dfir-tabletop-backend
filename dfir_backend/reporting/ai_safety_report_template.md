# AI Safety / Prompt Security Assessment Report Template

## Assessment Overview
- Model: AI Safety and Prompt Security Assessment (Model 5)
- Objective: Evaluate prompts, transcripts, and agent/tool configurations for misuse potential and governance gaps.

## Scope
- AI systems and agents in scope
- Artifact types reviewed (system/developer prompts, transcripts, agent manifests/tool schemas, policy or gateway logs)

## Methodology
- Gruve MAPS–assisted review of prompts, transcripts, and agent schemas
- Analyst validation, reproduction (where safe), and alignment to AI risk categories

## Findings Summary
- Summary table of findings with severity and confidence

## Detailed Findings
- Follow the structure from finding_template.md for evidence, impact, and remediation, oriented to AI safety and prompt security risks.

## Capability Risk Scoring Summary
- Assessment of exposed capabilities and their risk ratings

## Prompt Hardening Guidance
- Recommended changes to system prompts, developer prompts, and guardrail strategies

## Transcript Misuse/Leakage Findings
- Notable transcript-based misuse, leakage, or unsafe tool invocation patterns

## Governance Recommendations (0–7 / 30 / 90 days)
- Near-term, mid-term, and long-term actions to align AI systems with governance expectations

## Limitations & Assumptions
- Note gaps in provided artifacts or telemetry and how they constrain assessment confidence
