## Rules of engagement (important)
1. No-fault learning environment: the goal is improvement, not grading individuals.
2. Discussion-based: no systems are touched and no live analysis will occur.
3. State assumptions: if information is missing, state what you assume and what you would request.
4. Time-boxed decisions: the facilitator may force decisions to keep the exercise moving.
5. Confidentiality: treat exercise discussions and outputs as sensitive unless agreed otherwise.

## What to prepare (pre-read)
Before the session, please be ready to answer at a high level:
- Who can authorize major containment actions (that may cause disruption)?
- Who approves external communications?
- What are the top 3 business-critical services (“crown jewels”)?

Optional (if available):
- Review your incident escalation path and severity classification process.
